About Havenmark

More than a landlord — we're your partner

Since 1989, Havenmark Homes has been committed to providing quality, affordable housing across England. We believe everyone deserves a safe, warm, and secure home — a place to truly belong.

We work hand-in-hand with local councils, charities and community groups to deliver more than just homes — we build vibrant, supported communities where people thrive.
